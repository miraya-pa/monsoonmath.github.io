Projects

This page lists some projects by Monsoon Math alumni we support.

  • Online Math Club

    "The Online Math Club aims to increase exposure to olympiad mathematics and advanced mathematics. With a team containing IMO medalists and Campers, we hold regular live sessions on various topics."

  • Harshikaa Agrawal's Competitive Programming Videos

    Harshikaa Agrawal is creating videos that explain solution approaches and intuitions to students starting out in competitive programming

  • Monsoon Math Blog

    The Monsoon Math Blog hosts expository math articles aimed at advanced high-school and college students. We welcome contributions and cross-posts.

  • Unofficial Training Camp

    Because of the IMOTC not being conducted due to the COVID crisis, here we are back again to conduct the unofficial online version of the IMOTC called the Unofficial TC 2022! We hope through this camp, the INMO awardees ( mostly the juniors) can socialise with other INMO awardees and (obviously) learn new maths! The teachers of this camp are professors, International Olympiads medalists, senior INMO awardees and of course the students of the camp too!
